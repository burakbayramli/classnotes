$$ Ly = f(x) $$

$$ y_p + y_c $$

$$ L(y_p + c_1y_1 + c_2y_2) $$

$$ = \underbrace{L(y_p)}_{f(x)} + \underbrace{L(c_1y_1 + c_2y_2)}_{0} $$

$$ =f(x) $$
